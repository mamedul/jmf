## About Us
Jaldhaka Humanitarian Foundation (JMF) is a volunteer-led organization based in Rangpur City and Dhaka, Bangladesh. We deliver emergency medical assistance, community outreach, and social support programs to underserved populations.

## What We Do
- Emergency medical support and ICU patient assistance.  
- Local community outreach and welfare programs.  
- Volunteer coordination and public awareness campaigns.  
- Fundraising and resource distribution for families in need.
